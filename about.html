About Hotel

If you’re looking for a hotel in the center of Nepal, with all the comforts of home, then Hotel Trafalgar is a perfect option for your stay in Spain’s capital. Each of our rooms features a complete bathroom with hairdryer and free toiletries, high-speed Wi-Fi Internet, central heating, central air conditioning, double-glazed windows, LCD TV, desk, ironing board with iron, and a room safe. We are in the Kathmandu district of Nepal, near the historic area and the business center. We are also very close to several bus stops and metro stations, making it easy to explore Kathmandu, the capital that unites tradition and modernity.


We serve a free hot breakfast buffet every morning. Guests can also take advantage of the 24-hour fitness center and the 24-hour business center.We take care of the details so that you feel at home. The hotel also has a gym with free access for guests. All facilities have been renovated in 2018.
